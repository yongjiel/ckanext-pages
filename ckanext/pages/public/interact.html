Open Government

Interact

Open.alberta.ca encourages citizens to explore and take advantage of the Government of Alberta’s data and publications to research, make evidence-based decisions, build apps, and create visual data.

Apps
Explore winning user-submitted apps from the Apps for Alberta contest, and other Government of Alberta apps.

Visualizations
View Government of Alberta data visualizations such as interactive maps, dynamic charts, and infographics.

Blog
Open government matters and we want to share our stories about open government. Hear from us and read about insights from the Open Government team at the Government Alberta and learn about upcoming events.

For Developers
The Government of Alberta is committed to publishing open data to third parties via data feeds through an application programming interface (API). The Government of Alberta encourages developers to deliver and innovate using open data
